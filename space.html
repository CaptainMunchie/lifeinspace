Gj 1214b - The Water world

Gj 1214b - The Water world

A ocean like planet that only has water and no land that is considered to be bigger and hotter than Jupiter's moon. This planet contains mainly water organisms that are passive unless threatened.

Gliese 436b - Defying The Laws Of Physics

Gliese 436b - Defying The Laws Of Physics

30 light years from earth day defies the law of physics. The gravity of this planet can be extreme heavy during the day and extremely light during the night. Having at least 14 season's with its hottest being 439 degrees and coldest being -800 degrees. This planet contains very few organism that are partially living and more adaptive to the constant change in environment.

55 Cancri E - A Diamond Planet

55 Cancri E - A Diamond Planet

40 light years away, this planet is twice the size of earth and believed to be only covered in diamonds. Due to the high compression of this planet's gravity and the dense minerals it produces, this planet mostly rain diamonds. Land to the mineral organisms and it is uninhabitable to all living creatures.

Hat-P-7b - Where It Rains Rubies And Sapphires

Hat-P-7b - Where It Rains Rubies And Sapphires

1000 light years away from earth, this planet contains high aluminium oxide found within the atmosphere. The planet is cover in colourful gems so clear the you even see the other side to the planet. A single flash of light shine on this planet will make it glow like a disco ball sharing it's minerals to other planets for at least 100 year lights.

Hd 189733b - Where It Rains Glass

Hd 189733b - Where It Rains Glass

70 light years away from earth, this planet is covered only in glasses that’s as sharp as knifes. It rains mass quantity of glasses that melts when in contact with the land and expand at least 1km/y, it will constantly grow as long as it receive any source of light for a long duration of time. Home to the mindless glass golems cannot die unless shattered.

Wasp-12b - A Planet That's Eating Up Light

Wasp-12b - A Planet That's Eating Up Light

94 light years away from earth, this dark planet from the outside eats light like a parasite. It has the ability to camouflage itself within space with its mysterious flog like atmosphere. Home to many vicious creatures that hunt for entertainment this planet is for those who can survive the constant dark lands. It's temperature reaches at least 4,600 degrees and changes to -4000 degrees.

Kepler-10c - A Mega-Earth

Kepler-10c - A Mega-Earth

560 light years from earth, this planet it 20 times larger than earth containing a lease 200 unique environments. An ancient planet that contains many mysterious living organisms with fascinating adaptive skills and 30 million species and counting yet to be discovered. It is believed to contain human-like organisms that are 4 times stronger and wiser to the earth people. This planet contains many mysterious power that defies earth's law.

Psr B1620-26 B - Almost As Old As The Universe

Psr B1620-26 B - Almost As Old As The Universe

1 million light years away from earth, this planet is believed to be at least 13 billion years old closer to when the big bang theory began. Some mysteries are shrouded within this planet and could contain information for the universe existed. This planet is clustered by more than 100,000 stars all fused with the powerful core's gravity like a magnet absorbing other planets that are 50 light year close.
